    CURRENCIES AND CORE STATS

    Reward pressure, storage, and pacing

    The app uses multiple tracked values, but Gloom is the core operational currency. Streak and mood act as longer-term state bridges across daily behavior.

    StatRoleHow It Changes
    GloomMain earn/spend currencyTasks, calendar, oracle, disappointment; spent on care
    XPSecondary progression reserveDaily Disappointment and calendar impact
    LuckdustRare streak / chamber currencyEvery 3rd streak day and some oracle outcomes
    StreakConsecutive progress daysDriven by meta.lastProgressDate
    MoodPer-pal emotional stateTasks, neglect, care pressure, and calendar impact

    NEEDS SYSTEM

    Slow decline, measured precisely

    The active Pal's care state decays over time. Three needs drain downward while plague rises upward toward a hard failure state.

    NeedStartHourly ChangeThresholds
    Hunger80-2.2Warn at 55 or below, critical at 25 or below
    Boredom80-1.4Warn at 55 or below, critical at 25 or below
    Mood60-0.8Warn at 55 or below, critical at 25 or below
    Plague10+1.1Warn at 45+, plague tone at 75+, full plague at 100

    CARE SYSTEM

    Maintenance actions and hostile randomness

    Care spends Gloom to counter real-time decay. The Dread Oracle adds a daily random outcome, and the Orbit Burden panel shows when calendar debt is spilling into maintenance.

    ActionCostEffect
    Feed3 Gloom+28 hunger
    Entertain2 Gloom+22 boredom
    ComfortFree+18 mood
    Sanitize4 Gloom-34 plague
    Cure5 GloomClears full plague state
     
    Oracle outcomes: +6 Gloom, +4 Gloom, +2 Gloom, +1 Luckdust, +2 Luckdust, Nothing, Nothing, Plague

    TASK CONSOLE

    Manual tasks only

    The Home screen lists manual and library tasks. Calendar events live exclusively on the Schedule page and are not projected into the task list. Completed items move into the hamburger drawer below the active list.

    Manual task schema:
{
  id,
  title,
  completed,
  rewardClaimed,
  createdAt,
  completedAt,
  source,
  intensity,
  socialWeight,
  energyCost,
  dreadLevel,
}

    Calendar pressure (overdue mood penalties, pal dialogue) still flows through getCalendarPressureSnapshot(state) independently of the task console.

    LOAD SYSTEM

    Four-part task weight translated into reward and pressure

    Load is one of the app's most important mechanics. It lets manual tasks and calendar events carry emotional and practical weight instead of behaving like flat checklist items.

    load = intensity + socialWeight + energyCost + dreadLevel
range = 0..20

manual gloom = 5 + floor(load / 2)
manual mood  = 6 + (load >= 12 ? 4 : load >= 6 ? 2 : 0)
    Load RangeGloomMood Boost
    05+6
    1-55-7+6
    6-118-10+8
    12-2011-15+10
     
    Worked example:
intensity    = 3
socialWeight = 2
energyCost   = 3
dreadLevel   = 4
load         = 12

reward       = 11 Gloom
mood boost   = +10

    Rated manual tasks show I / S / E / D pills plus Load N. Unrated tasks now show Not rated yet. Calendar tasks do not render the manual stat row.

    STATE ARCHITECTURE

    One persisted object, many tightly linked systems

    All runtime state is normalized into one JSON object and saved through the same mutation path. This keeps the architecture simple but intentionally cross-coupled.

    Core lifecycle:
loadState()
saveState()
setAppState()
ensureStateIntegrity()
prepareSessionState()
    • Normalization upgrades older saves to the current schema
    • Session prep updates open history and streak breaks
    • Seasonal state syncs on app open
    • Neglect and mood sync on app open
    • Overdue calendar pressure applies once per new day
    • Anti-achievements are evaluated from real usage behavior
