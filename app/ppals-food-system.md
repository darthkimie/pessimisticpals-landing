# PPALS FOOD SYSTEM
## 5 foods per Pal. 50 items total. Voice-locked, diagnosis-shaped, drawable.

Each food has:
- **Name** + cost tier
- **Quote** in the Pal's voice (the line shown when they buy or eat it)
- **Visual** for DK's drawing direction — shape and form, not photographic
- **Tag** — favorite / liked / neutral / tolerated. The food system can use these however it wants for trust, mood, or hunger restoration tuning.

Cost tiers (Gloom):
- **STAPLE** = 3 Gloom (cheap, repeatable)
- **TREAT** = 5 Gloom (effort)
- **SIGNATURE** = 8 Gloom (the one)

Canon foods marked **[CANON]** stay verbatim.

Voice rules: they/them pronouns. No em dashes. No diagnosis labels in the copy. Each Pal sounds like themselves and only themselves.

## DRAWING NOTES FOR DK

50 drawings. Doable as a phase if scoped per Pal in single sittings.

**Per-Pal session suggestions:**
- One Pal per session = 5 drawings per sitting. ~10 sessions total.
- Order suggestion: start with the Pal whose foods are visually most distinct from the others (Doolin or Winta — lots of sparkle and gold). Get the most "fun" drawing energy first while the project is still novel.
- End with whichever Pal you find emotionally hardest to draw (probably Yun or Brutus — their foods are quiet and repetitive). By then, the system will be running and you will know what works.

**Visual coherence rules:**
- Each Pal's color palette stays in their hue family. Brutus's foods are mostly browns and dark warmth. Yun's are pale and quiet. Doolin's are gold and saturated.
- Plates and bowls should feel like the Pal's. Brutus has chipped ceramic. Doolin has decorative pedestal dishes. Zenji has perfect geometric containers. Elbjorg has plain durable enamelware.
- Backgrounds: keep clean. Most foods can be drawn on a single colored or transparent surface. Composition focus is the food.

**Sizing:** match the wardrobe item style. Same canvas size, same general framing. Food slots into the same shop UI as wardrobe.

**File naming:** `pessimistic-pals/assets/food/<slug>.png`. Slug = `<palid>-<food-slug>`. Examples:
- `brutus-cold-pizza.png`
- `ahote-spark-candy.png`
- `yun-listenlight-honey.png`

The full slug list is in `js/data.js` — every entry's `slug` field is the filename.

## NOTES FOR THE FOOD SHOP SYSTEM (Copilot side)

When implementing the shop:

- The **tag** field (favorite / liked / neutral / tolerated) should be the source of truth for whether eating this food affects trust positively. Favorites = small trust gain. Tolerated = neutral. Liked = mild positive.
- Each Pal can buy any food, but visual cues should make their own foods more prominent in their personal shop view.
- Do NOT auto-tag a food as a Pal's favorite based on origin. Use the explicit tag. Brutus's STAPLE foods are some of his favorites. Doolin's STAPLE (Spotlight Salad) is tolerated, not favorite. The tag knows.
- Costs are suggestions. The shop system can re-balance them if economy testing shows imbalance. Voice and tags are canonical. Costs are tunable.
